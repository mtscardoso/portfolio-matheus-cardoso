The Architecture of Silence

[Intro / Instrumental]
(Distorted synth pulses that sound like a failing heartbeat. A low, dissonant piano chord repeats every four beats.)
[Verse 1]
The air in this room has started to solidify.
A gray static hums where the light used to be.I’m a little bit of hollow, a little bit of "why try?".
Searching for a pulse in a ghost I can't see.My thoughts are just dust drifting through a dead space.
A stranger is wearing the skin on my face.
[Pre-Chorus](Bass starts to growl; electronic percussion kicks in) 
The pressure is feeding the voice in my head.I’m drowning in shallows, hanging by a thread.
I try to ignite, but I’m soaked in the rain.Looking for a exit from this cycle of pain.
[Chorus]
(Heavy, distorted guitars; soaring, desperate vocals) 
I’ve become a statue in a world made of glass.Watching the seasons and the memories pass.
I’m sinking under the weight of a sky I can’t hold.The fire is out and the echoes turn cold.
It doesn’t even matter if I’m screaming for air.
I’m trapped in a room and there’s nobody there.
[Verse 2]
(Beat becomes more frantic/glitchy) 
I’m picking at the seams of a habit I can’t break.Counting every second of this beautiful mistake.
My reflection is mocking the things that I crave.
I’m just a spectator at the edge of my grave.
The distance between us is a canyon of lies.
I see the betrayal when I close my eyes.
[Bridge]
(Building to a climax; rhythmic, aggressive delivery) 
The walls are closing! The surface is breaking! I’m losing the grip on the life I’m not taking! SHUT OUT THE NOISE! SHUT DOWN THE FEAR! I’M DISAPPEARING BUT I’M STILL STANDING HERE! 
[Chorus Final]
(Full wall of sound; layered vocals) 
I’ve become a statue in a world made of glass.Watching the seasons and the memories pass.
I’m sinking under the weight of a sky I can’t hold.The fire is out and the echoes turn cold.
It doesn’t even matter if I’m screaming for air.
I’m trapped in a room and there’s nobody there.
[Outro / Finalization]
(Instruments cut out abruptly, leaving only the faint piano and static ) 
I’m confused by what’s real.I’m starting to fade.Just a shadow... Of the person I made.
(Fade to silence)
